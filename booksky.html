Rich Dad Poor Dad

ROBERT

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a personal finance and self-help book written by Robert Kiyosaki. It was first published in 1997 and has since become one of the best-selling personal finance books of all time. The book presents financial education and advice through the lens of Kiyosaki's two "dads."

WINGS OF FIRE

APJ ABDUL KALAM

Summary of the Book One of the most inspiring and popular autobiographies to read is Late Abdul Kalam’s Wings of Fire. In this book, the former president shares his personal experiences and minutest details of his life. It narrates his life, right from the former late president’s rise from humble beginnings to his vision for the country in the year 2020.

The Automatic Millionaire

David Bach

The Automatic Millionaire shows readers how to change their financial practices and even their lives, the simple and automatic way. The book begins with a powerful story about an average Canadian couple — he’s a low-level manager, she's a beautician — whose joint income never exceeds $55,000 a year, yet who somehow manage to own two homes debt-free, put two kids through college, and retire at fifty-five with more than $1 million in savings.
